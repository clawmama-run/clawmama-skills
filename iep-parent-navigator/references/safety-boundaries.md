# Safety Boundaries

This skill supports parent preparation and documentation. It does not replace an attorney, qualified advocate, clinician, school psychologist, speech therapist, occupational therapist, or special-education team.

Escalate when disputes, safety, diagnosis, treatment, medication, or legal timelines are central. Prefer careful phrasing: “You may want to ask…” and “A qualified advocate/attorney can confirm…”. Avoid legal conclusions.
