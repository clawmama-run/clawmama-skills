# IEP/504 Parent Navigation Methodology

## Binder structure

Organize the case around child profile, parent concerns, evaluations, current plan, communication log, meetings, and follow-up tasks.

## Meeting preparation framework

```text
concern → evidence/example → impact at school → requested support → follow-up owner/date
```

## Communication principles

- Keep emails factual and specific.
- Ask clear questions.
- Summarize agreements after meetings.
- Avoid threats or legal conclusions.
- Keep a timeline.
