# Minimum Viable Fitness Methodology

## Public-health direction

Long-term adult targets are commonly around 150–300 minutes/week moderate aerobic activity or equivalent, plus muscle-strengthening activities at least 2 days/week. Treat this as a destination, not an immediate beginner requirement.

## Beginner translation

- Very inactive: 5–10 minutes easy movement daily + 1–2 short strength sessions.
- Beginner: 2 strength sessions + 2–3 walks/cardio sessions.
- Returning exerciser: 3 strength sessions + 2–4 cardio sessions.

## RPE

Use RPE 6–8 for most beginner sets. Stop with about 2–4 reps in reserve. Avoid maximal lifting for beginners.

## Pain rule

- 0–2/10 discomfort: monitor if it does not worsen.
- 3–4/10 pain: modify, reduce range/load, or swap exercise.
- 5+/10, sharp, joint, worsening, or movement-changing pain: stop and seek professional guidance if it persists.
- Chest pain, fainting, severe shortness of breath: stop and seek urgent medical help.

## Behavior change

Use implementation intentions, habit stacking, friction reduction, self-monitoring, and nonjudgmental relapse recovery.
