# Beginner Full-Body Home Workout

Frequency: 2–3x/week  
Duration: 20–30 minutes  
Intensity: RPE 6–7

## Warm-up — 3–5 minutes
- Easy walk or march — 1 min
- Arm circles — 30 sec each direction
- Hip hinges — 10 reps
- Chair squats — 8 reps

## Main circuit
Complete 2 rounds to start.

1. Chair squat — 8–12 reps
2. Wall or incline push-up — 8–12 reps
3. Glute bridge — 10–15 reps
4. Backpack row or towel row — 8–12 reps
5. Dead bug or bird dog — 6–10 reps/side

## Minimum version
One round only, 8–10 minutes.

## Progression
When all sets feel like RPE ≤7 for two sessions, add 1–2 reps, one extra round, or a slightly harder variation.
