# Missed Workout Repair

1. Do not double the next workout as punishment.
2. Choose one:
   - Do the workout tomorrow.
   - Do the 10-minute minimum version today.
   - Skip it and continue with the next scheduled session.
3. Identify the barrier: time, energy, soreness, forgot, equipment/location, motivation.
4. Adjust next week: reduce duration, change workout time, prepare gear, set reminder, or lower difficulty.
5. Reframe: one miss is data, not failure.
