# Job Search Methodology

## Principles

1. Targeted applications beat indiscriminate volume.
2. Every opportunity needs a stage, next action, and due date.
3. Resume tailoring must be grounded in truthful experience.
4. Referrals and networking are part of the pipeline, not a separate activity.
5. Weekly reviews improve conversion by detecting bottlenecks.
6. Rejections become data for strategy changes, not identity judgments.

## Fit score rubric

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Role match | 20% |
| Skill match | 25% |
| Seniority match | 15% |
| Location/compensation fit | 15% |
| Company interest | 10% |
| Referral path | 10% |
| Application effort | 5% |

## STAR/CARL story bank

Maintain stories mapped to competencies: leadership, conflict, failure, ambiguity, collaboration, technical depth, customer focus, ownership, prioritization, and influence.

## Ethics

Never invent qualifications. Never send messages/applications without confirmation. Never assist with interview or assessment cheating.
