# IELTS Methodology

## Writing criteria

- Task Achievement / Task Response
- Coherence and Cohesion
- Lexical Resource
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy

## Speaking criteria

- Fluency and Coherence
- Lexical Resource
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy
- Pronunciation

## Deliberate practice

Each session should produce a small improvement loop: attempt, feedback, error log, drill, retry, review.

## Score boundaries

All AI band scores are estimates. Do not guarantee outcomes. Use official IELTS resources for final rules, sample questions, and band descriptors.
