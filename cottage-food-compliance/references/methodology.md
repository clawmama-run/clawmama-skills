# Cottage Food Compliance Methodology

## Workflow

1. Identify jurisdiction.
2. Classify product and process.
3. Check storage/refrigeration and food safety risk.
4. Check sales channel: direct, online, shipping, wholesale, farmers market.
5. Check permit/registration, labels, sales cap, records, and renewal.
6. Prepare official verification questions.

## High-risk flags

- Refrigeration required
- Meat/seafood/dairy-heavy products
- Low-acid canned foods
- Acidified foods/pickles/sauces
- Interstate shipping
- Health or nutrition claims
